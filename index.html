What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a new cryptocurrency created in 2008 - 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto. No one controls bitcoin and they aren’t printed, like common currencies.

Conventional currency are based on gold, silver etc... Bitcoin it’s based on mathematics. People are use software programs that follow a mathematical formula to produce bitcoins. This process is called "Bitcoin mining". The mathematical formula is free and anyone can check it. Mining software is also open source, meaning that anyone can look at its source code to make sure that it does what it is supposed to or doesn't contains any kind of malware or virus.
